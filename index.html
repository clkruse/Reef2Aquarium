    Tracing the aquarium fish trade

    Start the Journey

Hundreds of millions of reef fish are plucked off the world's reefs each year and shipped across the globe to either perish along the way or land in someone's office or home. Our team has spent the past two years documenting this supply chain from start to finish. In both the Philippines and Indonesia, we have lived with, photographed, filmed, and interviewed ornamental fishers, middlemen, exporters, importers and hobbyists who drive this international trade. Now we are sharing the story where you will embark on this epic journey from the perspective of a fish.
